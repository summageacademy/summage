IELTS Reading Overview

In today's lesson, we will explore the main parts of IELTS Reading.

1. What Is IELTS Reading?

IELTS Reading gives you 3 passages and 40 questions. You have 60 minutes to read and find answers to the questions.

  • Texts come from books, ads, journals and even magazines
  • Topics range from science to history to education

🎯 Your goal: Understand the main ideas, opinions and details.
Make sure to stay focused during the exam

💥 2. Structure at a Glance

Section Type Text Length Focus
Passage 1 Simple & factual ~700 words Everyday topics
Passage 2 Moderate ~800 words Work or education
Passage 3 Difficult & abstract ~900 words Academic ideas

P.s: Difficulty increases gradually. So warm up with Passage 1 and keep going.

Mini Challenge

“You have 15 minutes for Passage 1. How would you divide your time between reading and answering?”

3. Scoring System

You’ll answer 40 questions, each worth one mark. Your total converts to a Band Score (1–9).

Raw Score Band Score
409.0
37–388.5
35–368.0
30–327.0
23–266.0

🎯 Aim for 30+ correct answers to hit Band 7. That’s the golden zone for most universities!
