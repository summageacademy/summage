Lesson 2: Common Part 1 Topics

Hometown, Hobbies, Family, Routines

Part 1 Topics Overview

In this lesson, we explore common IELTS Speaking Part 1 topics. These are simple questions about your personal life, routine, and interests.

1. Hometown

Questions often focus on where you live or grew up:

  • What kind of place is your hometown?
  • Do you like living there?
  • Has it changed in recent years?

🎯 Tip: Give a short description and include a personal opinion or feeling.

2. Hobbies & Interests

Be ready to talk about your free time:

  • What do you do in your free time?
  • Which hobby do you enjoy the most?
  • Have your hobbies changed recently?

🎯 Tip: Mention why you like your hobby and give a brief example.

3. Family

Expect questions about your family members and relationships:

  • Who are you closest to in your family?
  • Do you spend a lot of time together?
  • Has your relationship with your family changed over time?

🎯 Tip: Keep answers positive, short, and descriptive.

4. Daily Routines

Be prepared to describe your regular activities:

  • What is a typical day like for you?
  • Do you prefer mornings or evenings?
  • How do you balance work, study, and leisure?

🎯 Tip: Use sequence words like “first,” “then,” “finally” to make your answer clear.
