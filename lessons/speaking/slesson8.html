Sample Cue Cards for IELTS Speaking Part 2

Cue Card Practice Overview

In this lesson, you'll get sample cue cards for IELTS Speaking Part 2 and learn how to structure your responses.

Describe a memorable trip you took.

  • Where you went
  • Who you went with
  • What happened
  • Why it was memorable

Describe a person who inspired you.

  • Who the person is
  • What they did
  • How they influenced you
  • Why you admire them

Describe a book you recently read.

  • What the book is about
  • Why you chose it
  • What you learned
  • Would you recommend it?
