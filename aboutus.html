About Us

At Summage Academy, we believe education is not just knowledge—it’s freedom. Founded with a mission to make learning accessible, creative, and barrier-free, Summage connects students, teachers, and lifelong learners from Uzbekistan and beyond. We focus on English language mastery, skill-based learning, and youth empowerment, while maintaining a clean, modern, and inspiring study environment.

Our academy is built by passionate educators and innovators who understand that true learning comes from curiosity, creativity, and collaboration. Whether you’re preparing for the IELTS exam, exploring career opportunities, or building new skills, Summage Academy provides the tools and community you need to grow with confidence.

Our Mission & Values

Our mission is simple — to transform education into an experience that’s inspiring, practical, and truly global. We strive to build an academy that doesn’t just teach — it uplifts.

  • Accessibility: Education for every student, regardless of background or budget.
  • Innovation: Modern learning tools, gamified systems, and real-world projects.
  • Creativity: Encouraging every learner to think beyond the classroom walls.
  • Community: Building connections between students, teachers, and dreamers.
  • Purpose: Preparing youth to lead with wisdom, skill, and integrity.
