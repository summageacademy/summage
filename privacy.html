Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 25, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Summage Academy (“we,” “our,” “us”). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our website. We are committed to respecting your privacy and maintaining transparency about how data is used — especially as we collaborate with trusted partners like Ezoic to display family-friendly ads and improve your browsing experience.

2. Information We Collect

We do not require users to register, sign in, or provide personal details to access Summage Academy. However, certain information may be collected automatically for analytical and advertising purposes.

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent, device type, browser type, and general geographic region.
  • Cookies: Small text files stored on your device to improve functionality, performance, and personalized ads.
  • Third-Party Data: Ad networks such as Ezoic or Google may collect anonymous identifiers or browsing behavior to deliver relevant, non-invasive ads.

3. Use of Information

We use the limited information collected for the following purposes:

  • To analyze website performance and improve educational content,
  • To maintain site security and prevent fraudulent activity,
  • To display contextual and non-intrusive ads through trusted partners like Ezoic,
  • To understand how visitors interact with our site so we can keep improving.

4. Cookies and Similar Technologies

Cookies are small data files placed on your device when you browse. They help us enhance usability and measure how well our pages perform. You can manage or disable cookies anytime in your browser settings. However, some features may not function properly if cookies are disabled.

Our ad partners (Ezoic, Google AdSense, etc.) may also use cookies to deliver relevant ads and analyze traffic. These partners operate under their own privacy policies and comply with GDPR, CCPA, and global privacy standards.

5. Advertising Partners

Summage Academy partners with Ezoic to serve advertisements and provide analytics. Ezoic may use cookies, web beacons, and similar technologies to collect non-personally identifiable information such as browser type, device, time spent, and interests, in order to show you relevant and high-quality ads.

6. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to external resources, references, or partner websites. Please note that we are not responsible for the privacy practices of those third parties. We encourage you to review the privacy policy of every website you visit through our links.

7. Children’s Privacy

Summage Academy’s educational content is designed for general audiences, including teenagers and adult learners. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age. If you believe a child has provided personal data to us unintentionally, please contact us immediately, and we will delete the data promptly.

8. Data Retention and Security

We do not store any personal user data. Anonymous analytics or ad data collected through Ezoic and other partners is securely processed and retained only as long as necessary for legitimate business or compliance purposes.

We follow reasonable technical and organizational measures to safeguard any non-personal information processed through our site.

9. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access or request deletion of your personal data,
  • Opt out of personalized advertising,
  • Withdraw consent to cookies or tracking,
  • File a complaint with your local data protection authority.

You can manage ad personalization or opt out of cookies anytime via your browser settings or the Ezoic consent banner on our website.

10. International Visitors

Summage Academy is based in Uzbekistan, but our visitors may come from all over the world. By accessing our site, you acknowledge that your data may be processed in accordance with this policy, and that our partners may operate globally to provide reliable ad delivery and analytics.

11. Policy Updates

We may revise this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect updates in our practices, partnerships, or legal obligations. The latest version will always be available on this page, with the updated date noted above.
