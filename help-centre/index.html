Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Summage Academy

Is Summage Academy really free?

Yes! All courses, lessons, practice tests, and materials on Summage Academy are 100% free forever. We believe world-class education should be accessible to everyone, without any paywalls or subscriptions.

Do you offer official IELTS certificates?

No, we do not issue official IELTS certificates. We provide high-quality preparation courses to help you achieve your target band score. You'll need to register for the official IELTS test through British Council, IDP, or Cambridge.

Can I use Summage on my phone?

Absolutely! Our platform is fully responsive and works perfectly on phones, tablets, and computers. You can study anytime, anywhere.

How is Summage different from other platforms?

Unlike most platforms, we are completely free with no hidden fees. Our content is created by experienced IELTS instructors, and we focus on real exam strategies that actually work.

Will there be more courses in the future?

Yes! We're constantly adding new content. Follow our updates to be the first to know about new courses and features.
