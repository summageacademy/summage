We actively monitor for invalid traffic including bots, fake subscribers, automated views, or artificial ad clicks. Detection of such activity may result in reduced rates, suspended earnings, withheld payments, or permanent removal from the monetization program.

Eligible bloggers can view earnings statistics, traffic analytics, and other tools in their dedicated Blogger Dashboard.
