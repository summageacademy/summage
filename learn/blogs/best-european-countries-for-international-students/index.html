Best European Countries for International Students

Explore European Countries with The Best Universities and Scholarships.

Europe is one of the best regions for International Students as there are thousands of scholarships available and many of them cover tuition fees and monthly living costs. On top of that, some offer to cover even the travel expenses.

In this blog post, you can find the list of the best countries for international students with available scholarships.

Germany

It is by far one of the best options for international learners as it has many public universities and high quality education system. On top of that, there is a strong job market so you do not have to worry about financial burdens.

If you are looking for scholarship in Germany, DAAD is your strongest choice. They give monthly stipends of approximately 1000$ and fund travel expenses and insurance, too. This is the best option globally too, not just inside Europe!

United Kingdom

This country is popular for its top universities and strong global recognition. Most of the universities here are listed among the best universities globally. Prestigious universities like University of Oxford and University of Cambridge are located in United Kingdom.

One of the best scholarships in United Kingdom is usually listed as Chevening Scholarships. It offers monthly stipends, covers full tuition and even flights and visa expenses. It is said Chevening Scholarships covers expenses and tuitions for one-year master's for professionals with leadership potential.

And if you are from United States, Marshall Scholarship might fit you. American citizens willing to study in UK full-time might be eligible for a Marshall Scholarship. Every year, this organization gives up to 50 scholarship positions.

Many say there is a high competition but UK is a powerful choice for those learners with leadership abilities.

France

Here in France, the tuition fees are much more affordable and there are many English programs. France is usually ranked 2nd in Europe for education quality with 27 universities in the top 1000 global rankings.

France is also known for exceptional quality of life and cultural exchange opportunities. Many students enjoy rich cultural experiences outside school life. France has lots of heritage sites and museums so festive student life is available for learners.

For scholarship opportunities, Eiffel Excellence Scholarships are available. It offers monthly allowance of around 1400$ and it covers travel and insurance expenses.

Sweden

Sweden is often seen as innovation hub and it has many english programs for international learners. Sweden strongly focuses on sustainability and strong technologies. The education quality here is as excellent as other major European countries.

Swedish Institute Scholarship is the best choice if you have been looking for fully-funded positions. It covers full tuition fees of a student, offers monthly stipend and give travel grants.

Netherlands

Netherlands has many programs in English for students from overseas and the environment itself is international. It offers quality education and has lots of scholarship programs like Holland Scholarship and Orange Knowledge Program. Scholarships give many grants and offers so you better apply to universities in Netherlands too.

Switzerland

Studying in Switzerland offers particularly STEM and business related world-class education. There are globally ranked universities like ETH Zurich, EPFL and other research focused institutions. This country is also ranked as top global innovator and provides lots of opportunities fro students.

The education in Switzerlend can be much more different than traditional learning. In the country, programs often combine study with paid internships.

Swritzerland is also one of the safest countries in the world so you do not have to worry about safety issues. The country is located in central Europe and offers truly international atmosphere.

Hungary

Hungary is a top choice as it offers affordable but high-quality European education. It is located in Central Europe. It is listed as very safe country and it has generous scholarships like the Stipendium Hungaricum, which is ideal for international students.

Compared to Western Europe, Hungary offers much more affordable education without quality drop. Monthly living expenses are also lower than most European countries. It is located in the Schengen Zone, so traveling to other European countries is much easier.

For international students, The Stipendum Hungaricum scholarship is a massive advantage as it offers full tuition coverage and gives monthly stipends. You might also want to add universities here in Hungary to your plans too.

Europe-Wide Scholarships

Now, let's take a look at scholarships that is accessable for the whole Europe.

The Erasmus Mundus

The Erasmus Mundus Joint Masters scholarship is one of the prestigeous scholarships in Europe. It is often stated as fully funded program for international students, specifically to those who want to pursue master's desgrees at multiple European universities. Those who got in will receive generous monthly allowance of around 1600$ for up to 24 months. It also covers tuition, travel and visa fees.

Erasmus+ Mobility Grants

Erasmus+ Mobility Grants are specifically to students who already enrolled in a higher education institution in Europe. This program provides grants for study periods or traineeships of 3 months to 12 months.
