40 Daily Phrases in English for Beginners

Most Useful Phrases for New Learners

The best way to learn and apply english into your life is simply by learning basic phrases and using them frequently. Today, we have made a list of 40 useful phrases in english.

Greetings

1. How's it going?

You can use it to ask someone how they have been lately.

2. Nice to meet you.

You can use it when you meet someone new and you want to say that you are happy getting to know them.

3. Glad to meet you.

Also used to show that you appreciate meeting someone new.

4. Long time no see.

You can use it when you meet someone who you haven't seen in a while.

5. What have you been up to?

Use it when you want to ask someone about their life and news.

6. It's good to see you!

Used to show that you are happy to meet someone.

7. Hope you are doing well.

Way of saying that you hope someone is in a good state and winning in life.

8. How have you been?

To ask someone how they were feeling or what they were up to recently.

Asking Questions

9. Could you repeat that?

Question for asking someone to repeat what they just said.

10. What do you mean?

To ask someone what they were trying to say when you did not understand at first.

11. Can you help me?

To ask for help from someone.

12. What do you think?

To ask someone for their opinion on particular thing.

13. Do you mind if I ask something?

Ask this question before asking personal or private question to show respect.

14. Are you sure about that?

Ask this question when you are trying to know if someone is certain about something.

15. Where are you from?

Use it when asking someone about their origin, particularly their homeland.

16. Can you explain that again?

To express that you need more clarification about something.

17. What was that?

To ask someone what they just said. It can also be used when something happened suddenly and you have no idea about it.

Expressing Opinions

Use these phrases to express and deliver your opinions and thoughts about something.

18. In my opinion...

19. I think that...

20. Personally, I believe...

21. From my point of view ...

22. As far as I know...

23. To be honest ... / Honestly ...

24. I feel like ...

25. I would say that ...

26. It seems to me that ...

27. If you ask me ...

Agreeing & Disagreeing

28. I completely agree ...

You use it when you agree with someone about their opinion.

29. That makes sense.

To express that someone's opinion or idea is actually solid.

30. You are absolutely right.

To say that someone is right, that their opinion or idea is correct and makes sense.

31. I see your point.

Use it to let someone know that you understand them.

32. I'm not sure about that.

Use it to show that you are uncertain about something or someone's opinion.

33. I don't really think so.

Use it to show that you have different opinion on certain thing or topic.

Daily Conversations

34. I'm running late.

To express that you do not have any time and you are in a rush.

35. Take your time.

To assure someon that they do not need to rush and you are patient.

36. No worries.

To show that there is no need to worry about anything or something specific.

37. That sounds great!

To show that you like the idea of someone.

38. It depends.

A phrase that is used before giving certain answer, because you do not know a lot about something and there might be 2 outcomes for you.

39. I have no idea.

Use it when you do not know about something that is asked about from you.

40. Let me think about it.

Use this phrase when you need time before answering something.
